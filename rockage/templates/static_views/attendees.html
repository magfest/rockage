Attendee Tickets

Although we have lots of different ticketing options, a regular attendee ticket is still an all-access, three-day pass to everything at {{ EVENT_NAME_AND_YEAR }}. This includes all of the things listed below:
  • console gaming
  • tabletop gaming
  • 3 stages of music
  • artists / vendors
  • arcade cabinets, all on free-play
  • entry into tournaments
  • panels, presentations, contests, and game shows
SJSU Student Discounts:
San Jose State University students receive a discounted 3-day price. You must present a valid SJSU student ID for each student ticket purchased. If you don't have a valid SJSU student ID, you will need to pay full price at the door. This discount only applies for San Jose State University students, and does not apply to students from other schools.

You can also kick in extra to become a Supporter and get various bits of swag as thanks.
